Academics

From the stage to the science lab to the service-learning project, at St. Bernard Academy, our students leave here ready to thrive in their next academic endeavor.

Whether it's our students' longstanding success at math, science, and forensic competitions, perennially high scores on placement tests, or the high schools that vie for our students because of their maturity, preparation, and study habits, our priority is to help students succeed.

Our Learning Goals

At St. Bernard Academy, we believe that the success of our students is a direct result of the learning goals our teachers and administrators strive to help them achieve.

Spiritual Growth

Personal Wellness

Communication Skills

Expanding Knowledge

Interpersonal Skills
