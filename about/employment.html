Substitute Teacher

St. Bernard Academy (Nashville, TN) St. Bernard Academy is now accepting applications for substitute teachers for grades junior kindergarten through 8th level.
Teachers will provide detailed lesson plans along with all necessary information about classroom and school routines and procedures. At St. Bernard Academy, our talented faculty and staff are the backbone of our commitment to provide a culture for learning and development which supports a high level of achievement and success for each student.

We seek employees who desire to further our mission of providing an excellent, Catholic academic education built on a foundation of discipline, compassion, and a sense of responsibility for social justice in the tradition of the Sisters of Mercy. 

Accredited by the Southern Association of Independent Schools (SAIS) and a member of the National Catholic Education Association (NCEA), we are proud of our rich heritage and of being named as one of Nashville’s Top Workplaces by the Tennessean. In 2017, we were also recognized by the United States Department of Education as a Blue Ribbon School under the designation of High Performing Schools.
