Summer Program

Our summer program, Summer Play Days, helps to enrich our students’ knowledge and further enhance their SBA experience through themes, art projects, games, and more! Summer programs at SBA are open to children from all schools.

Summer Play Days

Join us for a summer of fun, games, water days, visitors, and plenty of play! We will have special guests throughout to help us explore new topics and opportunities. You don't want to miss it!

Summer Play Days 2024 Dates

June 3-7
June 10-14
June 17-21 (closed Wednesday, June 19 for Juneteenth)
June 24-28
July 8-12
July 15-19
July 22-26
 

Hours of Operation

Summer Play Days takes place daily from 8:00 A.M. - 4:30 P.M.

Cost

The cost for Summer Play Days is $220 per student, per week, with a daily drop in rate of $50 per student. Payment can be made in cash or check and a deposit of $50 per child is due upon registration. Individual camps have set their own prices and are due by the first day of camp; weekly Summer Play Days fees will be prorated for children that attend camp.

Registration

Click here to register for Summer Play Days! Registration is not considered complete without a $50 deposit and a copy of current immunization records. Please note that certain camps require an additional registration. Specific information can be found in the Camps section below, or you can email Jordan Harkey with any questions.
