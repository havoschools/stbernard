Service-Learning

At St. Bernard Academy, we recognize the importance of serving others in our local and global communities and strive to connect our students’ head, heart, and hands through various projects throughout the year.

Each level, and many of our clubs and activities, incorporate some sort of service-learning component into their curriculum. We are proud to partner with many well-respected Nashville organizations who also recognize the importance of serving others.

The character of an SBA student is evident in the way they go about their lives, showing compassion for those in the world that are in need of mercy.

Colleen Mooy
Upper School Teacher
mother of michael '26 and john '25
