Virtual Registration

Welcome to St. Bernard Academy's 2023-2024 Virtual Registration!

General information and sign-ups for SBA activities and services are below. Please read the directions carefully as submission information varies. If you have any questions regarding registration, please reach out to the appropriate contact{s) as indicated. Technical questions can be directed to Katie Wood. 

school e-mail communications

School e-mail communications including Bear Facts and other special notices are sent via our website communications platform. If, for some reason, you have not been receiving school communications and would like to ensure that you are added, please complete the form below.

If you are already receiving our e-mail communications, you do not need to complete this form. If you simply need to update your contact information, please contact Katie Wood.

important website links

SBA News

The best way to remained informed is to read our weekly parent newsletter, Bear Facts. Bear Facts is emailed every Thursday afternoon. If you have not received Bear Facts, please email Katie Wood to update your contact information. Additionally, don't forget to follow us on Instagram and Facebook. 

NEW SBA Parent Portals

We are thrilled to announce a new product supporting St. Bernard Academy, Veracross. Veracross is a multilateral platform that will support the day-to-day running of classroom schedules, the academic record keeping for our students, the entire application/re-enrollment process for families, and a new password-protected portal access for SBA Parents! These portals are almost ready. Please keep your eye on your inbox soon for this announcement and for your login information.

SBA Parent Webpage

Meanwhile, the SBA Parent Webpage is your current source for SBA parent resources and information including forms, links to other parent logins, and calendar info.

Home and School Association (HSA)

The St. Bernard Academy Home and School Association (HSA) is a parent organization dedicated to the successful education of your children through volunteerism and financial support of the school. Every parent or guardian at SBA is automatically a member of HSA. Information on the 2023-2024 SBA Home and School Association, including this year’s executive committee and a letter from HSA President Sam Creamer, is now available on the HSA page. Check this page often for HSA updates, and follow the SBA HSA Instagram to stay in the loop!

bus service

Bus service is available to and from SBA beginning on the first full day of school. Pick-up and drop-off are available from Brentwood (Brentwood Library) and East Nashville (Eastwood Christian Church). To sign up for the SBA Bus Service please fill out the form. Please Note: Due to the limited seating, the bus service is on a first come, first serve basis. Once your form has been submitted, you will receive a confirmation email from the office stating that you have a secured a spot(s). 

Questions? Contact Leona Floyd.

aftercare

Aftercare information for the 2023-2024 school year, including calendar and rates, will be posted here and on the Aftercare page.

Questions? Contact SBA Aftercare Director, Jordan Harkey.
 

paying for bus service or aftercare

Community Pass will be the system that we use for Aftercare and Bus billing. Within this system you will be able to pay aftercare and bus invoices as well as get receipts for tax purposes. New parents will need to register students. Returning parents will just need enroll their students for the new school year.

Girl Scouts

We are enrolling girls in existing troops and forming new Girl Scout troops at St. Bernard! Wherever your passions lie, you’ll find a place for them at Girl Scouts of Middle Tennessee. There’s no one “type” of Girl Scout. The only thing you should be is you. Whether you feel most yourself in the science lab, on the hiking trail, behind a camera, at center stage, or anywhere in between, Girl Scouts has activities that speak to your talents and skills. 

If you are interested in joining or simply learning more, contact one of the volunteers below.
*Two volunteers and at least 5 girls are needed to form a troop.  

Contact Information for Existing Volunteers

For questions about SBA's:

  • K-1st Level Daisy troop, contact Katelyn Burkhart Baker.
  • 3rd Level Brownie troop, contact Corinne Schaffer. 
  • 7th Level Cadette troop, contact Corinne Schaffer. 
  • For inquiries about forming other grade levels, contact Tracy Rokas. 

Cub Scouts

Calling all adventurous spirits — SBA’s Cub Scout Pack 96 is recruiting new students in Levels 1-5! Both boys and girls are welcome to participate. Our year long pack schedule consists of camping outings, hikes, a neighborhood clean-up, a holiday cookie party, and of course the Pinewood Derby. Interested Scouts and parent volunteers should reach out to Cubmaster, Justin Paschal, at  justin.lael@gmail.com.

music city arts academy

This year, Music City Arts Academy is pleased to be offering private music lessons taught by specialists in the following areas: piano, voice, drum, and violin. MCAA offers twelve (12), 30-minute lessons a semester; start date will be announced after school begins.  Space is limited so registration is on a first come, first served basis. To sign up for private music lessons:

  • Review the 2023-2024 MCAA Student Policies
  • Download and complete the registration form by clicking the button below
  • Send in the completed registration form and fee (via check or Venmo) to Renee Thompson

Questions? Contact Renee Thompson.
