For over 150 years, we have provided a rigorous and stimulating academic experience to young minds in Nashville. Our inclusive and independent Catholic identity is not only unique to the Nashville community, but also to the state of Tennessee and throughout the southeast.

From their successes in the classrooms, on the athletic fields and the stage, to the science lab, and in the art studio, SBA students continuously leave their mark on this historic institution, taking their knowledge and talents to a diverse selection of high schools — and out into the world. They quickly become examples of the quality and value of the SBA experience and education.

We invite you to make an appointment for a campus tour to come see why we are no longer the “best kept secret in Nashville." We look forward to seeing you soon!
